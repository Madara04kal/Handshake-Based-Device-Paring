How it works

  1. 01

    Pair

    Open the Sender console and pair your browser with Board A over Bluetooth.

  2. 02

    Shake

    Start the handshake, then hold both boards together and shake them as one.

  3. 03

    Compare

    Board A and Board B swap motion data over ESP-NOW and check that it lines up.

  4. 04

    Paired

    A matching generated key confirms both boards are paired — no PIN, no typing.
